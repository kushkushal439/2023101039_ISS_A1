Achievements

JEE Mains:
99.9 percentile

JEE Main is a national-level entrance exam in India for undergraduate engineering programs. Conducted by NTA, it tests candidates in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The scores are used for admission to top institutions like IITs and NITs

Scored exceptionally good in my board examinations

In India, board exams (CBSE or state boards) are pivotal, shaping a student's academic journey, influencing college admissions, and serving as competitive benchmarks. Success marks a significant milestone, opening doors to higher education and setting the foundation for future careers.

Cleared NTSE

NTSE, or the National Talent Search Examination, is a competitive scholarship program in India for Class 10 students. Administered by the NCERT, it aims to identify and support exceptional talent by providing scholarships for higher education. Successful candidates receive recognition and financial assistance, fostering academic excellence and talent development.

Getting into IIIT-H

IIIT Hyderabad excels in technology education and research in India. With a focus on information technology and computer science, it offers diverse programs. Known for research, strong industry ties, and modern facilities, IIIT Hyderabad provides a dynamic education in technology.

Scored 330 in BITSAT

BITSAT, the Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test, is a computer-based exam assessing students in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Logical Reasoning, and English Proficiency. It's the key to prestigious engineering programs at BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad, emphasizing academic knowledge and critical thinking skills.
